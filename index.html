Anaís is an artist, organizer & body-worker whose practice is informed by somatic experiencing, body-mind centering, dance, traditional Chinese medicine & their spirituality. they support QTBIPOC with their mind/body/spirit attunement as a source of calm, power, inspiration and resilience. they believe in the collective revolutionary potential of our bodies as a point of radical care, rest & resistance.

Bren Denommee, aka Bricklayer, is a dj & visual artist currently based in Brooklyn. a long time raver & a cofounder/resident of the Boston-based queer collective Boudoir, they are a strong believer in the healing power of the dancefloor & the community-building potential of nightlife. their selections cross multiple genres & eras of thumping basslines, ranging from classic house & acid to sci-fi soundtrack electro & techno to driving disco & funk.
